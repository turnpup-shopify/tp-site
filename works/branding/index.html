htgt furniture

This work was done as a side project for my parent's furniture store, which is located in an old World War II quonset hut in Southern California. Their store is known for selling one of a kind antiques and accessories at affordable prices. I felt that it was important to illustrate that Here Today Gone Tomorrow is a funky, design-savvy store when doing the branding for the store.
