    Google Ads

    • As of May 2021, Caraway allocates approximately $60,000 monthly to Google Ads, resulting in $193,462 in generated revenue, showcasing a solid return on ad spend (ROAS) of 3.3. From these efforts, they net $18,455 per month in profits through Google Ads. Despite their success, Caraway is still in a growth phase and has the potential to increase their advertising expenditure significantly. The cautious approach might stem from a deliberate pace or due to returns not meeting their desired benchmarks yet. Anticipating substantial growth in both Search and Shopping, Caraway's potential expansion into YouTube Ads seems promising once they refine their strategy further.
