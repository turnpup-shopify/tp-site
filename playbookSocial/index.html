Omnichannel

  • Reels: Instagram’s algorithm continues to push video content - I’ve gained hundreds of followers through successful Reels I’ve posted (sometimes I boost these with paid media, too)
  • Product Seeding: I’ve grown accounts by gifting product to influencers when they organically post about the gift
  • Proactive Outreach: Commenting on other brand’s/influencer’s content has helped my brands grow when the comment gets lots of engagement
  • Partnerships: Very thoughtful co-brands pushed through social media are leading to lots of growth for brands like Deux
  • Giveaways: A bit more old-school at this point, but giving away product by asking people to follow you, comment, tag a friend, etc. can lead to growth (though oftentimes people will unfollow you right after)
